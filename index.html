A version of this plot appeared originally in Dave Arnold's Liquid Intelligence: The Art and Science of the Perfect Cocktail. The data is his and you should buy his excellent book.

Popular Science still has an excerpt of Dave's book. Unfortunately, the server that had this plot appears to be dead and the file doesn't seem to be mirrored anywhere. This page was reconstructed with the aid of the quantities in Dave's book (pp. 130-135). In the printing of the book I have, some of the numbers are formatted incorrectly. I've used round estimates of these to match the printed figure rather than recalculating. There are like transcription errors and I noticed the size of circles for blended drinks don't match the book. The printed finish conditions are the same as the initial, so perhaps the text doesn't reflect the ice? I've also added links to the excellent NCOTW posts on /r/cocktails.
